Potateos and Peas in curry

►

Ingredients

Chicken
1kg

Wild garlic
12g

Salad leaves
250g

Cinnamon Sticks
75g

Onion
500g

Tomato
50g

  • Cooking time: 25 min
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • servings: 05

Instructions

  • STEP 01

    Place lemon Juice, lemon zest, garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt and pepper in a
    large, non-reactive bowl. Whisk to combine.

  • STEP 02

    Preheat oven to 425F. Remove chicken from marinade and lplace in a single
    layer in a large baking dish, skin side up.

  • STEP 03

    Bake for 30 minutes and baste the chicken pieces generously with the
    reserved marinade.

  • STEP 04

    Grill the slices until crispy brown, and the chicken is cooked through, juices
    running clear.

  • STEP 05

    Pour the juices from the pan into a serving bowl. Use a tablespoon to skim
    the fat off the top. Enjoy!
